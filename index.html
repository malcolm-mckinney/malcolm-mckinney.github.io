About Me

Hello again — Malcolm here!

I enjoy a mix of domestic (Japan) and international travel, art and history museums, learning Japanese, fine dining, wine tasting, and, as you can see, most recently winter sports! Feel free to drop me an email or send me a message on LinkedIn if you would like to get in touch with me!
